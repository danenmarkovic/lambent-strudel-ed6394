Cookie policy

How Bion Med uses cookies

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files or similar browser technologies that help a website remember choices, keep basic functions working, measure performance or support marketing activity.

Essential cookies

These are required for the site to function, remember your cookie preference and support basic security or form behavior. They cannot be switched off through the preference panel.

Functional cookies

These may remember preferences such as interface choices, saved settings or embedded service options. They are optional and used only if selected.

Analytics cookies

These may help Bion Med understand how visitors use the site, which pages are useful and where the experience can be improved. They should be aggregated where possible and are optional.

Marketing cookies

These may support campaign measurement, remarketing or cross-context advertising. They are optional and should not be enabled unless the visitor accepts them.

Your choices

You can accept all cookies, reject all non-essential cookies or choose individual categories when the cookie preference banner is shown on the site.

Bion Med uses essential cookies required for the website to function and to remember visitors' cookie preferences. Non-essential cookies, such as analytics, marketing or third-party tracking cookies, are not used unless they are clearly listed in this Cookie Policy and accepted by the visitor through the cookie banner. Visitors can refuse non-essential cookies and can change their cookie choices at any time.
